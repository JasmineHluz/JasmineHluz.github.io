About Domestic Violence

Type of Abuse

There are many types of domestic violence. There is: Physical, Sexual, Emotional, Psychological, and Financial. Physical violence is an attempt to hurt another through physical harm, but can also be used to limit someone's self determination. Sexual violence is an attemot to harm someone through sexual acts. Emotional violence is a way for someone to use emotions from their victims to manipulate them. Psychological violence is to use someone's psycological integrity through threats. Financial violence is when a partner controls the other's access to finances, this makes the victim dependent on the other partner.

More

Domestic violence can be in all relationships. Can be in dating, marriage, parent and kids, etc. It also isn't just men that are the agressors, just most of the time. If you sense that something is off in the way your partner treats you, speak up, you may be at risk for domestic violence. This type of violence affects kids by sometimes being at risk of being the aggressor, and it harms their physical and mental state. It is important to stop domestic violence because we can prevent many deaths, mostly of women, and no one deserves to die that way.
